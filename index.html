Here are a few of the projects I am most proud of.

  • Stat-That

    Real Time Volenteer connects students with there needed Community service credits. It digitaly sends you time stamped dnd signed certificates of attendance with Geo Location Data.   • Battle Axe

    Battle Axe is a platformer that used paralaxing and Dynamic sound reproduction to turn a 2d game into a 3d world. It did this by Using Dynamic Sound Reproduction to give every object in the X,Z, and In the visible but  unaccessible Y Axis a sound and Purpose. This recieved most Entrepreneurial at Code Day SF 2015 Spring.

  • Math VR

    Math VR is a Math and physics simulator that allows you to Visualize hard topics like launching a rocket from the moon to get to earth. It may sound like a simple task but it requires you calulate for trajectory, moving objects and physics problems like resistance. This claimed a honorary mention (we were too young to recieve cash/scholarship prize) at At&T VR Hackathon 2015.
